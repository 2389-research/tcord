Features

Built for the way thoughts actually happen

One-tap recording

Tap the button on your Apple Watch and start talking. No app switching, no fumbling. The interface is one button because that's all you need.

AAC · 22kHz · mono

Instant transcription

Apple Speech runs directly on your Watch the moment you stop recording. No internet needed. Your words become text before your arm drops.

on-device · private

Nothing gets lost

Both devices keep your recording until it's confirmed in the cloud. Durable queues with automatic retry. Your notes survive dropped connections, restarts, and dead zones.

retry · persist · sync

Browse on iPhone

All your notes land on your phone with full transcription text. Tap to read, play the original audio, copy, or share. Swipe to delete.

read · play · share

Background sync

WatchConnectivity transfers your recordings in the background. You don't need to open the iPhone app. It just arrives.

WatchConnectivity

Your data, your silo

Sign in with Apple. Every recording and transcription is stored under your user ID with Firebase security rules. Nobody else can access it.

firebase · isolated
How It Works

Three seconds from thought to text

Record

Tap the button on your Apple Watch. Talk. Tap again when done. That's it.

Transcribe

On-device speech recognition turns your voice into text instantly. No cloud, no latency, no privacy concerns.

Sync

Audio and text transfer to your iPhone and up to the cloud. Both devices retain until confirmed. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Under the Hood
